About Me

Feeling a nagging need for a career change, I decided to pivot my trajectory from the veterinary field (as a technician) to the web developer field. I am currently enrolled in a full-stack bootcamp program through Triology Education Services at Washington University here in Saint Louis, Missouri.


I am enjoying learning about this new world of programming and all the opportunities it has to offer. Seeing the interactive world through this different persepctive brings about entirely new levels of thinking and creativity.


Feel free to take a look around my growing portfolio!

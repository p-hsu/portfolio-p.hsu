ABOUT ME

Feeling a nagging need for a career change, I decided to take a 180˚ pivot from veterinary medicine to the web developer field. I am currently enrolled in a full-stack bootcamp program through Triology Education Services at Washington University here in Saint Louis, Missouri.

I absorb with facination every new skill learned and every new perspective gained. With discoveries of a passion behind human-centric design and community-driven projects, I look forward to creating that which can bring about real changes in the world. My meticulous attention to detail and organization serves as a reliable resource for efficient and clean work.

Persistence in a ever changing world:
I won't forget where I started - but I'm far more eager to look at where I want to go.

When I am not in school or working, I enjoy spending time with my partner and cat, rock climbing, cooking, and baking.
